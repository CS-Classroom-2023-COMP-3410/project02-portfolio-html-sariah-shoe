About the Book

Title: Unconventional Family

Progress: First Draft

Description: This story takes place in a mountaintop temple dedicated to the goddess Alene. When Samuel's wife, Celine, dies, the temple mandates he marries Celine's sister, Aldith, and her husband, Justice. The story follows Samuel's journey of healing from grief and finding new love.
