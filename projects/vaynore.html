About the Book

Title: The Vaynore Cousins

Progress: Self Editing

Description: This story follows Ikeshia Vaynore. She and her cousin Ilphus are considered the most powerful magicians ever to exist but that power comes at a price. When they are betrayed by their country's council, Ikeshia starts on a path to revenge that will destroy everyone and everything around her.
